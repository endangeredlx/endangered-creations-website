About

Endangered Creations is a video and music production company built on a passion for visual storytelling. From concert stages to music videos, we bring artistic visions to life through cinematic production and creative direction.

Founded by Erica D. Hayes, Endangered Creations has produced content spanning live events, music videos, branded content, and documentary projects. Every frame is crafted with intention, blending technical precision with bold creative vision.

Based in Los Angeles, we work with artists and brands who demand visuals as powerful as their sound.
