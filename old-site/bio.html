About Endangered Creations

Endangered Creations Inc is a full-service music and video production company founded with a passion for creating unique, compelling content that pushes creative boundaries.


From music production and artist development to video direction and live event production, Endangered Creations brings a distinctive vision to every project. Our work spans across multiple genres and mediums, collaborating with talented artists and brands to deliver exceptional results.


Based on a foundation of artistic integrity and innovative thinking, we continue to evolve and create content that resonates with audiences worldwide.
